Welcome to the official app for the Yemeni community in Ankara, Turkey.

This application provides essential services and information to help you navigate life in Ankara.

Key Features:
- Stay updated with the latest community news and events.
- Access a directory of local businesses and services.
- Get important notifications directly to your phone.
- Connect with other members of the community.
- Find helpful resources for students and residents.

Whether you are new to the city or a long-time resident, this app is your companion for staying connected and informed.
